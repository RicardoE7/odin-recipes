Fudge Recipe

A fudge recipe with only 3 easy ingredients!

The BEST fudge recipe your family will EVER taste. Sweet, chewy, chocolatey and absolutely delicious. You and your family will never go back to any other recipe once you try this one!

Ingredients

  • Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  • Condensed Milk
  • Vanilla Extract

Steps

  1. Line a 9" x 9" pan with foil or parchment paper and mist. with cooking spray
  2. Place chocolate chips and condensed milk in a bowl and combine.
  3. Microwave for 1 minute and stir.
  4. If the chocolate isn't smooth, microwave for another 30 seconds (as needed).
  5. Fold in the vanilla until combined.
  6. Pour smooth chocolate into the pan and refrigerate for at least an hour.
  7. Cut into 1" x 1" squares and enjoy
